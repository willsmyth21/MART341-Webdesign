Ramen Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 packages or instant ramen noodles
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 cup diced onioin
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • tablespoon of vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Cook ramen noodles, 3-4 minutes
  2. Add vegetable oil to large pan
  3. Add additonal ingredients to pan
  4. Heat on medium stirring often, allow oil to fry ingedients
  5. Stir in cooked ramen noodles in pan
  6. Allow ramen noodles to cook in pan for a few minutes
  7. Enjoy!

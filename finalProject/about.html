ABOUT US

Inside.vt is a visual technology studio creating the latest and most creative of visual art pieces. A team of 10 and growing, members range in specialties to create diverse works of multimeda collaboration. Anything from paintings or graffiti to fashion and sewing, to graphic motion and still design along with everything in between. We work anonymously under various aliases. Due to controversial works of art and creations that upset others, the group went anonymous to allow full freedom of expression.

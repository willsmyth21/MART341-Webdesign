Italian Sausage:

Ingredients

  • Hot Italian Sausage
  • Fettucine
  • Red Pasta Sauce
  • Quarter of an onion
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Bell Peppers
  • Garlic Powder

    • Cooking

    Sausage

    Place two sausages in pan with enough water to cover bottom of pan. Place lid on pan and let steam on medium heat until water is gone. After water is gone flip sausage until fried to preference.

    Pasta

    Boil water in medium sized pot. After water is to a boil place two servings of fettucine in pot. Add pinch of salt and cook for 12 minutes on medium heat. Stir often

    Sauce

    Cut half a pepper and dice. Dice a quarter of an onion. Add two servings of pasta sauce to small pot. Add onion and pepper along with desired amount of pepper flakes and garlic powder. Cook on low heat for a few minutes until simmers.

    Final Preperation

    Drain pasta in colander and place back in pot. Cut sausage into pieces of desired size and add to pot with pasta. Pour sauce into pot and stir everything together until well mixed. Serve yourself and enjoy a delicous quick meal!
